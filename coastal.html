Discover the Coast

The Redlands Coast offers a range of coastal experiences for the whole family to enjoy. Take the ferry across to Straddle and have a day trip exploring the local culture and crystal beaches or head to the coastline for an evening picnic looking over the water.

The Activities

When exploring the South-East coast there is so much to do as a family. Go fishing, snorkelling, diving, surfing, stand up paddling or even kayaking across the water. For people who prefer to stay a little dry the Redlands also offers boating and cruising opportunities for whale watching or just a fun day out.
