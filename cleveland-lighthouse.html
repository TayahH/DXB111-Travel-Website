Cleveland Lighthouse

Set up a picnic in the evening and enjoy the sunset as it falls upon the historical Cleveland lighthouse

A Bit About the Lighthouse

Wrapped in ocean views of Moreton Bay and the beautiful parkland of Cleveland Point, Cleveland Lighthouse offers a range of family fun opportunities. Located right at the tip of Cleveland the Lighthouse, built in 1864, is an exciting landmark of Redlands history.


Whilst the lighthouse is not open to the general public visitors can still admire the refurbished form of the unusual hexagonal tower and its history whilst also enjoying the beauty of the Cleveland coastline.

What to Do?

Clevelands offers a range of activities for the family to enjoy. Discover the history of the Cleveland Lighthouse, enjoy a picnic along the foreshore, take a stroll along the point and end the day with a meal from the Lighthouse restaurant.
