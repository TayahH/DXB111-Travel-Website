About Us

Our Mission

At South-East Local we aim to allow people to reconnect with themselves and the people around them. Focusing on the family fun and shared experiences that can be had throughout the Redlands we aspire to create lifelong memories.

Showcasing the exciting events and locations throughout Redlands City, South-East local is the one-stop destination to find new family adventures and experiences to have.

Where Are We?

Located in Brisbane's South-East we are only a short 30 minute drive down from Brisbane's CBD, and an hours drive up from the Gold Coast.
