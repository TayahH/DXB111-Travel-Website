Sirromet Winery

Enjoy eachothers company with a meal, glass of wine and stunning country views

A Bit About Sirromet

Sirromet is a family owned and operated winery located within Mount Cotton. With state of the art facilities Sirromet offers food and wine lovers as well as the whole family the ultimate experience. With a range of activities and eating opportunities you can be guaranteed a fun time. Along side the usual activities Sirromet often hosts external events at their venue. Weekend park runs and the “cars and coffee” events, as well as their renowned “Day on the Green” where musicians from around the globe come to perform.

What to Do?

Sirromet hosts numerous activities for everyone to enjoy. With their fine dining restaurant the whole family can relax with a meal whilst the adults enjoy a luxury wine. For those old enough Sirromet offers wine tasting tours, however if looking for a day out with the whole family they offer Super Golf, a modified form of golf with oversized clubs and balls to ensure a fun day of entertainment.
