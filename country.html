Explore the Country

If you’re planning a visit to the South-East you can’t pass up an experience to explore the natural beauty of the countryside. Embark on a bushland adventure through relaxing bush walks, mountain bike tracks, horse riding trails and other activities to rekindle your connection with nature.

The Wildlife

The Redlands Coast is inhabited by a diverse range of local wildlife living amongst the community. By exploring the South-East, visitors can immerse themselves in the local environment, discovering over 170 different species of flora and fauna thriving in the local area.
