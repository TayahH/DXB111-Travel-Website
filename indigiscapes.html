Indigiscapes

Adventure amongst the wildlife and endulge in the serenity of the Redlands natural environment

A Bit About Indigiscapes

Situated on 14.5 hectares of natural bushland Indigiscapes Centre is Redland City Council’s environmental education centre. Offering native botanical gardens, nurseries, bush trails, playgrounds and a café socialising in local cuisine.


Whether you are seeking a relaxing family day out, searching for inspiration for your own native garden or are passionate about sustainable and environmentally friendly living, Indigiscapes can educate and inspire you to explore outdoors more whilst assisting in improving the natural environment.

What to Do?

When visiting Indigiscapes there is so much to do. Relax in the beautiful grounds, go for a walk and discover the natural beauty of the Redlands Coast, get involved in community groups and activities, or take a rest and enjoy your surroundings in the café.
